About Dr. Varun Sundarrajan

With 18 years of experience after training at the prestigious Christian Medical College, Vellore, I have a passion for children's health and provide comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. I am committed to providing the highest quality care in a compassionate, friendly, and patient-focused environment.

My services range from preventive health care to diagnosing and treating acute and chronic diseases. I believe in empowering parents with knowledge and ensuring our young patients grow up healthily. "Every child matters."
